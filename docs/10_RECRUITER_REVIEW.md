# Recruiter review lens

## Strongest differentiators

1. Production AI is paired with evaluation rigor, not only prompt usage.
2. Backend ownership includes rollout, operations, and incident judgment.
3. The profile contains measurable business and system impact.
4. Research experience is directly relevant to trustworthy AI systems.
5. The education background supports both engineering and quantitative evaluation.

## Main narrative to protect

Youfu should not look like:

- a student trying to become a software engineer
- a frontend developer who recently added AI keywords
- an AI researcher without production ownership
- a long list of AWS services without a coherent specialty

He should look like:

> A production software engineer who can design, evaluate, launch, and operate
> AI-enabled backend systems.

## 30-second test

After reviewing the home page, a recruiter should be able to say:

- Amazon SDE with 2+ years of production ownership
- AI/ML and backend focus
- credible AWS experience
- clear evidence of reliability and impact
- research recognition
- ready for mid-level interviews
