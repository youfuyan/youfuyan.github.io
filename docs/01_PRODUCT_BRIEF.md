# Product brief

## Audience

Primary:
- recruiters for Software Engineer II / mid-level SDE roles
- hiring managers for backend, platform, AI infrastructure, and GenAI product teams
- engineers evaluating technical depth before an interview

Secondary:
- research collaborators
- conference readers
- former coworkers and referral contacts

## Positioning

Youfu is a production software engineer with an unusually useful combination:

- end-to-end software ownership at Amazon
- GenAI/ML evaluation and inference experience
- distributed backend and AWS production experience
- frontend experience sufficient to ship complete products
- formal training in computer science, statistics, and quantitative methods
- peer-reviewed LLM + knowledge-graph research

## Core message

`I build production AI and backend systems that ship.`

The site should emphasize judgment, ownership, reliability, and measurable outcomes.
It should not present Youfu as a person who merely knows many technologies.

## Target roles

- Software Development Engineer II
- Backend Software Engineer
- AI/ML Infrastructure Engineer
- Generative AI Engineer
- Platform Engineer
- Full-stack engineer on an AI product team

## Recruiter scan

Within 15 seconds, a visitor should see:

- current role
- target technical areas
- three concrete impact signals
- two or three selected case studies
- a resume link
- LinkedIn, GitHub, and email
